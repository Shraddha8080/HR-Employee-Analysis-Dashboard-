# HR Employee Analysis Dashboard — Power BI

**Tools:** Power BI | HR Analytics | Employee Data | Business Intelligence

## Objective
Analyse employee data to uncover workforce insights including attendance, 
performance, attrition, and departmental trends to support HR decision-making.

## Dashboard Highlights
- Employee attendance and presence tracking
- Work-from-home vs office analysis
- Department-wise headcount and performance
- Attrition rate and retention insights
- KPI cards for quick executive overview

## Key Insights
- Identified patterns in employee leave and attendance behaviour
- Surfaced departments with high attrition risk
- Enabled HR team to take proactive retention actions

## Skills Demonstrated
- Power BI dashboard design
- HR analytics & workforce analysis
- DAX measures and calculated columns
- Data cleaning and transformation in Power Query
- Stakeholder-ready business reporting
